Encryption Task Earnings Instructions

Both Blue and Yellow players engage in the Encryption task for their additional earnings. For each word encrypted correctly, you will earn 2 tokens. You are free to earn as many tokens as you can during the 6-minute encryption task. You will earn nothing for any word encrypted incorrectly. Note that both Yellow and Blue players face the same conditions. So, each player earns 2 tokens for each word encrypted correctly.

However, there is one additional condition that depends on the roles. For each word your matched Blue player encrypted correctly, you (as the Yellow player) lose 1 token from your earnings. But, the reverse is not true: the number of words encrypted correctly by the Yellow player has no effect on the earnings of the Blue player.

As you are the Yellow player, your earnings are positively affected by your performance in the encryption task, but negatively affected by your matched Blue player’s performance in the encryption task. Note that the Blue player was also told that their performance would negatively impact the earnings of the Yellow player before they began the encryption task.

On the next screen, you will be provided with three different scenarios of what impact your performance and your matched Blue player’s performance will have on additional earnings.

Encryption Task Earnings Examples

Suppose both you and your matched Blue player were able to encrypt 20 words correctly. This means that your earnings from the Encryption task will be (20 × 2) = 40 minus (20 × 1) = 20 = 20 tokens. Hence, in this example, the matched Blue player would earn (20 × 2) = 40 tokens, while you would earn 20 tokens.

Now, suppose you were able to encrypt 20 words correctly, while your matched Blue player was able to encrypt 25 words correctly. This means that your earnings from the Encryption task will be (20 × 2 =) 40 minus (25 × 1 =) 25 = 15 tokens. Hence, in this example, the matched Blue player would earn (25 × 2 =) 50 tokens, while you would earn 15 tokens.

As a last example, suppose you were able to encrypt 20 words correctly, while your matched Blue player was only able to encrypt 5 words correctly. This means that your earnings from the Encryption task will be (20 × 2 =) 40 minus (5 × 1 =) 5 = 35 tokens. Hence, in this example, the matched Blue player would earn (5 × 2 =) 10 tokens, while you would earn 35 tokens.
