Encryption Task Earnings Instructions

Both Blue and Yellow participants engage in the Encryption task for their additional earnings. The Encryption task lasts for 6 minutes, and you are free to make as much money as you can. For each word encrypted correctly, you will earn 2 tokens. You will earn nothing for any word encrypted incorrectly. Note that both Yellow and Blue players face the same conditions. So, each participant earns 2 tokens for each word encrypted correctly.

However, there is one additional condition that depends on the roles. The Blue player will have been randomly assigned by the computer program to have one of two payoff schemes prior to participating in the encryption task. Depending on which payoff scheme your matched Blue player was assigned to, your earnings may be negatively affected by their performance in the encryption task.

The computer program has randomly assigned the Blue player to one of the following payoff schemes:

  • For each word encrypted correctly, 2 tokens are added to the Blue player earnings but the Yellow player loses 1 token.
  • For each word encrypted correctly, 1.5 tokens are added to the Blue player earnings with no effect on the Yellow player’s earnings.

As you are the Yellow player, your earnings are positively affected by your performance in the encryption task, but might be negatively affected by your matched Blue player’s performance in the encryption task depending on the random assignment of the computer program. Note that before the Blue player began the encryption task, the Blue player was also told how their performance would impact the Yellow player they would be matched with.

On the next screen, you will be provided with three different scenarios of what impact your performance and your matched Blue player’s performance will have on additional earnings if the Blue player did get assigned the payoff scheme that would decrease your earnings.

Encryption Task Earnings Instructions

The following are earnings examples only if the computer program matched you with a Blue player whose payoff scheme would decrease your earnings.

Equal Performance

Suppose both you and your matched Blue player were able to encrypt 20 words correctly. This means that your earnings from the Encryption task will be (20 × 2) = 40 minus (20 × 1) = 20 = 20 tokens. Hence, in this example, the matched Blue player would earn (20 × 2) = 40 tokens, while you would earn 20 tokens.

Better Performance by Blue Player

Now, suppose you were able to encrypt 20 words correctly, while your matched Blue player was able to encrypt 25 words correctly. This means that your earnings from the Encryption task will be (20 × 2 =) 40 minus (25 × 1 =) 25 = 15 tokens. Hence, in this example, the matched Blue player would earn (25 × 2 =) 50 tokens, while you would earn 15 tokens.

Better Performance by Yellow Player

As a last example, suppose you were able to encrypt 20 words correctly, while your matched Blue player was only able to encrypt 5 words correctly. This means that your earnings from the Encryption task will be (20 × 2 =) 40 minus (5 × 1 =) 5 = 35 tokens. Hence, in this example, the matched Blue player would earn (5 × 2 =) 10 tokens, while you would earn 35 tokens.
