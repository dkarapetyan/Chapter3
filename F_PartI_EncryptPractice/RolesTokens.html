Roles
If you are a blue player, each word you encrypt correctly will take away 1 token from the earnings of the yellow player you are paired with.

If you are a yellow player, your earnings will decrease by 1 token every time the blue player you are paired with correctly encrypts a word. You will not take away any tokens from the blue player for the words you encrypt.

Token Earnings

Each 5-letter word block encrypted correctly will add 2 tokens to your score. All letters within the 5-letter word block need to be encoded correctly to earn any tokens for a word.

If any letters within the 5-letter word block are encoded incorrectly, you will not earn any tokens for that word.

You will now have a chance to practice the task for 2 minutes. You will not be paid any additional payment for the practice.
