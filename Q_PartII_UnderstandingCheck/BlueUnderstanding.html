Part 2: Encryption Task Instructions


The first task in Part 2 will be the same encryption task as in Part 1. In this task, you will be shown a 5-letter word that you will encode into 5 numbers. After you finish encoding a word, the computer program will prompt you with another word to encode. Once you encode that word, you will be given another word, and so on.

This process will continue for 8 minutes (480 seconds).

Player Roles
There is a possibility that each word a blue player encrypts correctly may decrease a yellow playerâ€™s earnings by 1 token.

Similar to Part 1, each word a yellow player encrypts correctly will not take away tokens from the blue player.

Token Earnings
Each word a player encrypts correctly will add 2 tokens to their earnings.

If any letters within a word are encrypted incorrectly, no tokens will be earned for that word.

The maximum number of tokens any player will earn is 64. To earn 64 tokens, 32 words would need to be correctly encrypted.
