{{ block title }} Part 2: Encryption Task Instructions {{ endblock }} {{ block content }}

Each word a yellow player encrypts correctly will add 2 tokens to their earnings, and will not take away tokens from the blue player.

As the blue player, you will have a decision to make in your token earnings for Part 2. You will choose to either earn 2 tokens for each word encrypted correctly while taking 1 token away from the yellow player OR earn 1.5 tokens for each word encrypted correctly without taking any tokens from the yellow player.

If any letters within a word are encrypted incorrectly, no tokens will be earned for that word.

The maximum number of tokens any player will earn is 64. To earn 64 tokens, 32 words would need to be correctly encrypted.
