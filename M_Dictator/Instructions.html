The decision task will be conducted as follows:

You are now endowed with 30 tokens, and the blue player is now endowed with 15 tokens.

As the yellow player, you will now decide what portion, if any, of this 30 tokens to transfer to the blue player you were paired at the beginning of this study. You can also transfer a negative amount: i.e., take up to 15 tokens from the blue player in 5 token increments.

Your take home earnings from this study will be the sum of your participation fee for completion of the study, your encryption task bonus payment, and the bonus payment from this decision exercise.

Likewise, the earnings of the blue player will be the sum of their participation fee for completion of the study, their encryption task bonus payment, and their bonus payment outcome from this decision exercise.
