Thank you for completing the Encryption task. Recall that in that task, you were matched with a Blue player who had the opportunity to reduce your earnings from that task. We now turn to the Decision task.

In this task, you (as Yellow player) are matched with the same Blue player you were matched with for the Encryption task. However, now you have an opportunity to change their earnings.

In this task, you have been provided 30 tokens, while your matched Blue player has been provided 15 tokens. You have the opportunity to transfer tokens to, or take away tokens from, your matched Blue player. You can transfer a positive amount in 1 token increments up to a total of 30 tokens, or you can take away/transfer a negative amount 1 token increments up to a total of 15 tokens.

The transfer/take away examples will be provided in the next screen.
