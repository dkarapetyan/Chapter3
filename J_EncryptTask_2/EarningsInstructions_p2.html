Part 3: Encryption Task Earnings Instructions

As the Blue player, you will now have a decision to make about your earnings and the earnings of your matched counterpart. You will choose to either earn 2 tokens for each word encrypted correctly while taking 1 token away from your matched Yellow player OR earn 1.5 tokens for each word encrypted correctly without taking any tokens from your matched Yellow player.

For each word your matched Yellow player encrypts correctly, 2 tokens will be added to their earnings. And, once again, your matched Yellow playerâ€™s performance will not have any impact on your earnings in the encryption task.

Suppose you chose to earn 2 tokens, and both you and your matched counterpart encrypted 20 words. As an example, you would then earn (20*2=) 40 tokens and your matched Yellow player would earn (20*2=) 40 minus (20*1=) 20 = 20 tokens.

Now, suppose you chose to earn 1.5 tokens, and both you and your matched Yellow player encrypted 20 words. You would then earn (20*1.5=) 30 tokens and your matched Yellow player would earn (20*2=) 40 tokens.
