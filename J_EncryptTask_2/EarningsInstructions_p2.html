Part 3: Encryption Task Earnings Instructions

As the Blue player, you will now have a decision to make about your earnings and the earnings of your matched counterpart. You will choose to either earn 2 tokens for each word encrypted correctly while taking 1 token away from your matched Yellow player OR earn 1.5 tokens for each word encrypted correctly without taking any tokens away from your matched Yellow player.

For each word your matched Yellow player encrypts correctly, 2 tokens will be added to their earnings. And, once again, your matched Yellow player’s performance will not have any impact on your earnings in the Encryption task.

Part 3: Encryption Task Earnings Examples

Here are three different scenarios of what impact your performance and your matched Yellow player’s performance could have on additional earnings:

Suppose both you and your matched Yellow player were both able to encrypt 20 words correctly.

  • If you chose the payoff scheme that would have no effect on the Yellow player’s earnings, your earnings would be (20 x 1.5=) 30 tokens while the Yellow player would earn (20 x 2=) 40 tokens.
  • If you chose the payoff that would decrease the Yellow player’s earnings, your earnings would be (20 x 2=) 40 tokens while the Yellow player would earn (20 x 2=) 40 minus (20 x 1=) 20 = 20 tokens.

Now, suppose you were able to encrypt 25 words correctly, while your matched Yellow player was able to encrypt 20 words correctly.

  • If you chose the payoff scheme that would have no effect on the Yellow player’s earnings, your earnings would be (25 x 1.5=) 37.5 tokens while the Yellow player would earn (20 x 2=) 40 tokens.
  • If you chose the payoff scheme that would decrease the Yellow player’s earnings, your earnings would be (25 x 2=) 50 tokens while the Yellow player would earn (20 x 2=) 40 minus (25 x 1=) 25 = 15 tokens.

As a last example, suppose you were only able to encrypt 5 words correctly, while your matched Yellow player was able to encrypt 20 words correctly.

  • If you chose the payoff scheme that would have no effect on the Yellow player’s earnings, your earnings would be (5 x 1.5=) 7.5 tokens while the Yellow player would earn (20 x 2=) 40 tokens.
  • If you chose the payoff scheme that would decrease the Yellow player’s earnings, your earnings would be (5 x 2=) 10 tokens while the Yellow player would earn (20 x 2=) 40 minus (5 x 1=) 5 = 35 tokens.
